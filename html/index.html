A sand sculpture in support of the missing Malaysian airliner MH370, by artist Sudersan Pattnaik, sits on a beach at Puri Beach in India on March 12, 2014.

8 March 2014 - The fateful day

  • Flight Route: Kuala Lumpur international airport, Malaysia to Beijing international airport, China
  • Last communication: 01:19 MYT, 8 March; when it was over the South China Sea, less than an hour after takeoff
  • Last tracked:It disappeared from air traffic controllers' radar screens at 01:22 MYT.
  • Passenger count: 12 Malaysian crew members and 227 passengers from 15 nations.
  • Possible theories:Electronic hijacking, Spoofed saellite data, Fire, Shootdown-theory
  • Search locations: South-east Asia, Southern Indian ocean
